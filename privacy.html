Privacy Policy — Hushwave

Last updated: 1 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Hushwave content-publishing tools (the “App”), operated by the Hushwave channel (“we”, “us”), handle data when we use third-party platform APIs to publish our own original content. The App is a private tool used by the channel operator to upload videos that we create to our own connected accounts.

What the App does

The App takes video files and captions we have produced ourselves and uploads them to accounts we own and control — for example, sending a video to our own TikTok drafts for us to review and publish, or publishing a Reel to our own Instagram account. The App is not offered to third parties and does not act on behalf of other people’s accounts.

Information we access

  • Authorization tokens. When we connect an account (e.g. via TikTok or Meta OAuth), the platform issues access/refresh tokens. These are stored locally on the operator’s own machine and used solely to upload our content. They are never shared or sold.
  • Content we create. The video files, titles, captions, and tags we author for publication.
  • Basic account identifiers needed to target uploads to the correct connected account (e.g. an account/user id).

We do not collect, store, or process data about viewers of our content, and we do not access followers lists, private messages, browsing/viewing history, or other personal data beyond what is strictly required to upload our own posts.

Data obtained through the TikTok API

Any data obtained through TikTok’s Content Posting API is used only to upload our own videos to our own TikTok account (the “Upload to Inbox / drafts” flow). We do not use TikTok data for advertising, profiling, resale, or any purpose unrelated to publishing our content. Tokens obtained via TikTok are stored securely and can be revoked at any time (see “Your choices”).

How we use information

Solely to authenticate with, and publish our content to, the accounts we have connected. We do not use it for advertising, analytics about other people, or any unrelated purpose.

Third-party platforms

To publish and host content, the App transmits our videos and captions to the platforms we choose to post on, and to a private storage bucket used only to make a file fetchable by a platform during upload:

  • TikTok — privacy policy
  • YouTube / Google — privacy policy
  • Meta / Instagram — privacy policy
  • Google Cloud Storage (private bucket; short-lived signed links used only for one-time fetch during upload)

These platforms process the content under their own privacy policies once it is published.

Storage, security & retention

Authorization tokens and credentials are kept on the operator’s own systems and are not disclosed to anyone. Hosted video files are stored only transiently for upload (auto-expiring links; files are routinely deleted). We retain tokens only for as long as the connection is active.

Your choices

Because the App only touches accounts we own, control of the data rests with the account owner. A connection can be revoked at any time from the platform’s own settings (e.g. TikTok → Settings → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions), which invalidates our tokens. On request, we delete any stored tokens.

Children

The App is an internal publishing tool, not directed at children, and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date above will change accordingly.
